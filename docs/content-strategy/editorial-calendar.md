4. Add links from the two existing posts back into the definition post after it is live.

## Page-Level SEO Notes

Every post should have:

- A title under 60 characters where possible.
- A meta excerpt under 155 characters.
- One H1 that matches the article title.
- H2s that answer obvious follow-up questions.
- At least three internal links.
- A Cloudflare Images social card when the post becomes important enough to promote.
